So you want to start your career as a web developer, and you don't know how to go about it, worry no more because i got you covered. So im gonna be guiding you on how to go about it. FIRST THING FIRST....

CHOOSE YOUR STACK

It's either you are a software developer or a web developer, you gotta pick one. If you choose to be a software developer, you have to decide if you want to be a mobile app developer, or a desktop app developer. But if you choose to be a web developer, you have to decide to be either a UI/UX(User interface/User experience)Designer, FrontEnd or BackEnd Developer. To my greatest understanding no one is a Fullstack Developer in 2020. Reason being that the languages and frameworks are now too much for a single head to handle.

GET A PERSONAL COMPUTER: SPECIFICATIONS{MUSTHAVE}

Most enthusiast would like to start a career in Software or Web Dev, but dont actually have a PC and as well trying to do stuffs with their phones. Trust me using phones ain't bad but as i dive deep, you come to realize that the use of phone is limited technically as it can't perofrm specific actions. My little advice for you is to try your possible best to get a PC for programming.
